Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28 June 2025

Welcome to the JNTUH SGPA & CGPA Calculator (https://jntuh-calculator.vercel.app). We value your privacy and aim to provide full transparency about how your data is used on this platform.

1. What We Collect

We do not collect personal data. When you upload a result PDF, it is processed directly in your browser using PDF.js. None of your academic information is stored or sent to any server.

2. How Your Data is Used

The uploaded PDF is used temporarily in your browser to extract grades and credits, then calculate SGPA/CGPA. This data is discarded after you leave the page and is never saved.

3. Google AdSense

Our website uses Google AdSense to serve ads. AdSense may use cookies to personalize ads based on your interaction and preferences. Learn more on the Google Ads Policy page.

4. Cookies

We do not use custom cookies. However, Google AdSense and its partners may use cookies to deliver personalized ads and track ad performance.

5. External Links

Our website may include external links such as the JNTUH Results Portal. We are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of external sites.

6. Children's Privacy

This site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may occasionally update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. Please check back regularly for updates.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, contact us via our Telegram community at @JntuhR18notes.

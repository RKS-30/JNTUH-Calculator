JNTUH SGPA & CGPA Calculator

📘 What This Tool Is About

This website helps you automatically calculate your SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average), CGPA (Cumulative GPA), and overall percentage — just by uploading your result PDF. It’s fast, accurate, and handles all cases including backlogs, labs, projects, and more.

📥 How to Get Started

  1. 🔎 Visit the JNTUH results(vercel) portal: jntuhresults.vercel.app
  2. 🔎 Visit the official JNTUH results portal: jntuhresults official
  3. 🧾 Enter your Hall Ticket Number to view your result.
  4. 📄 At the bottom of your result page, click "Download PDF".
  5. 📂 Come back here and upload the downloaded PDF using the “Choose File” button below.
  6. ⚙️ Click “Process PDF” — and boom 💥! You’ll see your SGPA, CGPA, and percentage semester-wise.

✍️ No PDF? No Problem!

You can also switch to the Manual Input tab to add your subjects, credits, and grades manually. It’s flexible for any student who doesn’t have access to their PDF yet.

Built for students. Optimized for simplicity. 💙

🎥 Watch: How to Use the Tool

⚠️ Disclaimer:
This calculator is built to make your academic life easier — especially if you’re juggling backlogs or just want quick insights. It works hard to read your JNTUH result PDFs and crunch your SGPA, CGPA, and percentage based on the latest available data.

However, if you're still clearing some subjects, keep in mind that these results are best-effort estimations. Once all subjects are passed and officially processed by JNTUH, your final CGPA or percentage might vary slightly.

So use this tool for clarity and confidence — just remember, your official transcript always has the final say. 📜✨
📌 Important Note:
Please upload only PDF files that are downloaded from the official JNTUH Results Portal or the jntuhresults.vercel.app website.

❗ If the file is in any other format (like images or screenshots), or if it’s not the original downloaded PDF, the calculator may not be able to read your results correctly.

📘 Understanding JNTUH SGPA & CGPA Calculation

SGPA stands for Semester Grade Point Average and represents a student's performance for a single semester. CGPA is the Cumulative Grade Point Average that combines results from all semesters. JNTUH follows a credit-based evaluation system where each subject is assigned a weight.

To improve your CGPA, it’s crucial to focus on core subjects and lab components since they carry more credits. This tool is designed to simplify your academic tracking.

Remember, official transcripts will always provide final results. Use this calculator as a support resource.
